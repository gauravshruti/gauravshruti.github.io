Our Story

It all began in 1992 when the two became next door neighbours. After six fun years, came eight years of absence. Gaurav moved away with his family. 2006 was when Gaurav & his family were finally visiting their old home. To all their surprise, Shruti & family were home.

Shruti & Gaurav were able to reconnect during the summer of 2006 when Gaurav visited her for a few weeks. A friendship rekindled has become an anchor for their lives. They met again in 2007 & 2010, grew ever closer, and foolishly hid their feelings after. Years passed sharing their lives over letters. Time flew by...

2018, it was time. After years of dreaming, the two of them travelled together, touring Portugal & Spain. A majical trip of epic encounters reminded them of something forgotten yet not lost; their love for each other. They didn't hide their feelings any longer; and to both their surprise, the feelings are mutual. Last couple minutes of the trip, at the airport, Gaurav proposed to Shruti!
